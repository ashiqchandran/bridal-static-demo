About Me

Hello! I'm Maya, a professional makeup artist with over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry. My passion is helping people look and feel their best for any occasion.

Specializing in bridal makeup, editorial looks, and special event styling, I've worked with clients across Kerala to create beautiful, personalized looks that enhance natural features and reflect individual style.

I stay updated with the latest techniques and products to ensure my clients receive the highest quality service. Whether you're preparing for your wedding day or a special event, I'm committed to making your experience relaxing and your results stunning.
